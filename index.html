ashley's poetry

The concept of radical inconvenience means experiencing the discomfort of exposure to the process rather than purely striving for outcome, creating mental endurance. In this case, a little website was the exercise. Don't look at the code. It's not good.

Should it be a portfolio? Should it be a resume? Will you see me for who I am? Should I tear open the crafts I pursue, and-- with precision coordination-- piece them into a narrative that will garner fame and virality?

Will you love me then?

For now, it will be poems, and that is all. Thank you.

under this tree 09-19-2025
As the season came to an end--
supple beings overripe and rotting on the ground--
each slightly imperfect fruit, delicious in the end.
They were left to be consumed by this Earth.
I wait for the perfect bite.

The tree presents new bounty,
next season lesser in quantity than the last.
Yet, I wait-- hoping for perfection despite.

The tree glancing down and this wide eye child,
sitting at its trunk asking,
“Why are my fruit not enough for you?”
And it starts to creep to mind, was the perfect one already grown?
Was I paying attention when it happened?
Who ate you?

Maybe all that was to be grown has been grown.
Drink her fermentation as you wait until next,
expect hunger when those flowers bloom.

But this one tastes rotten.

Tall boy 01-17-2026
Dissonance in our timeline, my sweet revival.
To its natural conclusion,
To the puzzles and expansion.

I will ride this sweet wave
and patch each springing hole on this ship--
as mighty and fair
as this water may be.

What is there to be scared of?
Blurted out confessions?
Plumbers tape tangled on threads?

boards hastly glued to boards and overflow leaking out,
steering into a great sea with a water-logged compass.

God dammit.
Just let the water in.

Please, bravely moore yourself towards dry land.
This sideways rain, oh, it is a blessing.
Yes, so blessed are we in these moments of meditation.
Whisper your secrets into the sea as you float,
let these letters scatter and sink under our full moon.
